# AGENTS.md

This repository contains the specification and design guidelines for the **Feature-Agent-Spec** architectural pattern. It is primarily a documentation repository.

## Editing Guidelines
- Keep the YAML frontmatter intact in `README.md` containing `summary`, `read_when`, and `title`.
- Follow the versioning scheme in the document title (`(FEATURE_AGENT_SPEC-X.Y.Z)`) and log all additions, deletions, or modifications in the **Version History** table at the top of `README.md`.
- Ensure subsequent section numbers are updated and cross-references (such as Section 5.3 to 6.3) are maintained.
